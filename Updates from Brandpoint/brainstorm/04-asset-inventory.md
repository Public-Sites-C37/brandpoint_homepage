## Image Optimization Notes

Several provided PNGs are large (Native Ads 1.6MB, Specialty Formats 1MB, See Our Work 712KB). For production:
- Consider WebP conversion with PNG fallback
- Compress PNGs (lossy OK for photos, lossless for logos)
- Lazy-load images below the fold
- The logo PNGs (PR Agency, Outlets) have transparent backgrounds — good for the slider
- Some outlet logos have white backgrounds (Albany Times Union 117KB is notably large)
